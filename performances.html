Hire Tamashii Taiko Drummers

Whether you are looking for an explosive opening act for a large-scale corporate function or vibrant, engaging entertainment for a local community festival, we can tailor a performance package to suit your audience and venue.

Corporate Events & Premium Entertainment

Make a massive impact. Our premier performance squad delivers high-octane, visually stunning sets designed to command attention. Perfect for award ceremonies, product launches, gala dinners, and large-scale corporate entertainment.

  • Explosive Openings: Start your event with a literal bang to immediately energize the room.
  • Custom Choreography: A highly polished, visually striking performance that showcases deep discipline, precision, and power.
  • Professional Coordination: We work directly with your event planners to ensure seamless staging, sound, and lighting integration.

Community Festivals & Cultural Celebrations

We are deeply passionate about sharing the joy and energy of Taiko with the Auckland community. From street fairs and cultural festivals to school events and charity fundraisers, our scalable performance groups bring the festival spirit to life.

  • Engaging & Accessible: A vibrant, family-friendly performance that draws a crowd and gets people smiling.
  • Flexible Group Sizes: We can scale the size of our drumming ensemble to perfectly match your venue space and community event budget.
  • Interactive Elements: Ask us about incorporating short, interactive drumming demonstrations into our set to get your community involved!
