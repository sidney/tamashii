About Tamashii Taiko Drummers

Auckland’s Premier Contemporary Japanese Drumming Ensemble

Founded in 2007, Tamashii Taiko Drummers have spent nearly two decades bringing the thunderous energy of contemporary Japanese drumming to Aotearoa. Based in Auckland, we are a passionate, diverse collective of performers dedicated to pushing the boundaries of traditional Taiko through dynamic choreography and athletic movement.

A Legacy of Performance

Over the years, Tamashii has built a reputation for delivering unforgettable, high-octane performances. We have had the honor of taking our rhythmic energy to some of New Zealand’s biggest stages.

  • Major Milestones: Our performance history includes proudly playing at the Rugby World Cup, featuring on national television broadcasts, and opening for major international events.
  • Community to Corporate: From commanding the stage at high-end corporate galas to bringing the festival spirit to local Auckland community events, our adaptability and professionalism have made us a trusted entertainment partner for nearly 20 years.

Our Mission & Purpose

In 2022, Tamashii Taiko officially registered as a not-for-profit Incorporated Society in New Zealand. As a governed, community-focused organization, our primary mission is to promote Japanese taiko drumming and its related performing arts throughout Aotearoa.

Operating as a not-for-profit means that every performance we book, every corporate gig we play, and every class we teach directly funds our ability to share this incredible art form. It allows us to maintain our instruments, train our performers, and keep our community workshops accessible to all.

Our Unique Style

We honor the deep cultural roots of Japanese drumming while distinctly carving out our own contemporary space. "Tamashii" translates to "Soul" or "Spirit," and that is exactly what we bring to the stage. Our signature style blends traditional, heart-pounding rhythms with visually striking, athletic movements and modern choreography.
